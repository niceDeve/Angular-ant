Frequently Asked Questions


How many states does a task have ?

A task can have four states in different periods.

Analysing -- Engine is analysing magnet url in order to get necessary torrent information. This can take a long period.

Running -- Task is running.

Stopped -- Task has been stopped.

Completed -- Task has been Completed.

What is itorrents service ?

itorrents is a free service for caching torrent files online. ANT Downloader will always try to get torrent infomation from magnet url to reduce waiting time. If itorrents has no such infomation, ANT Downloader will use engine to resolve magnet url.

Can ANT Downloader support more protocols, like ed2k and baiduyun ?

Actually, i am thinking about it. However, my time is limited. I need get enough feedbacks to decide whether if ANT Downloader should support specific protocol. You are welcome to supply feedbacks if you have ideas.

Why is the speed of ANT Downloader lower than some Bittorrent clinets like xunlei ?

ANT Downloader can get the best theoretical speed. And speed depends on your network condition and popularity of resources. In some cases, some bittorrent clients like xunlei can have better speed since xunlei only share speed with their own users. You can use torrent or magnet url from websites like thepiratebay to solve this problem.
